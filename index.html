The problem

Security tools in place.
Structure missing.

Effective security is not achieved through more tools. It requires clear ownership, consistent standards, and executive visibility into what actually matters.

Most organizations aren't breached because they lack tools. They're breached because the structure that governs those tools was never built.

Our methodology
⚙
Tools without alignment EDR, SIEM, MFA in place - but not configured for your environment or monitored with consistent oversight.
🔗
Vendors without accountability PMS providers, OTA integrations, IT partners - all with network access, none with clear contractual security obligations.
📋
Standards that vary by site Each property or client environment operates differently. No baseline. No way to assess or compare risk across the portfolio.
👁
Leadership without visibility Decisions made without a clear picture of what's actually at risk - until an incident forces the conversation.
