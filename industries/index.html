The common thread

What every sector has in common

Regardless of industry, the organizations Abbott Cyber works with share a pattern: they've grown past the point where IT alone can own security, but they're not large enough to justify a full-time CISO. The gap between the two is exactly where a vCISO delivers the most value.

The regulatory landscape differs. The threat actors differ. The technology environments differ. But the need for executive-level security leadership - someone who can own the program, drive the roadmap, and translate risk into terms a board or ownership group can act on - is consistent across all of them.
