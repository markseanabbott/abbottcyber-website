M365 Secure Score

Most organizations are leaving significant M365 security settings disabled by default

Microsoft 365 ships with a Secure Score - a measurable indicator of your configuration against Microsoft's security recommendations. Most organizations that use M365 have never reviewed it. The findings typically include MFA gaps, legacy authentication still enabled, external sharing policies wider than intended, and alerts that aren't routing to anyone.

Penetration testing

Automated and manual pen testing - findings that drive action

Penetration testing is often misused: ordered because a compliance framework requires it, scoped too narrowly to be meaningful, and delivered as a report that gets filed without action. Abbott manages the testing engagement from scope through remediation - so the findings actually change something.

🤖

Automated Penetration Testing

Continuous or on-demand automated pen testing using purpose-built platforms that safely simulate real attack techniques across your network, endpoints, and cloud environment. Faster than manual testing, repeatable on a schedule, and capable of validating controls across your full environment rather than a spot-checked subset. Ideal for ongoing assurance between manual assessments.

Human-layer testing

Phishing simulations and security awareness

Email is consistently the most common attack vector. Phishing simulations measure whether your staff can identify and report suspicious email - not in training, but under realistic conditions. The results drive targeted awareness training for the staff who need it most, not blanket training for an organization that already knows what to look for.

Phishing simulations are most effective when they're tied to a security awareness program - not run as a one-time test that produces a click rate and nothing else.

Talk about phishing testing

  • Click rate and reporting rate measurement
  • Targeted training for staff who need it most
  • Security awareness program development
  • Integration with identity and email security controls
  • Ongoing simulation schedule to maintain awareness over time
