Regulatory context

What your clients' compliance requirements mean for you

As an MSP, you sit between your clients and their compliance obligations. When a client needs to demonstrate security controls for their cyber insurer, or satisfy a SOC 2 requirement from an enterprise prospect, or respond to a PIPEDA-related inquiry - you're often the first call.

Understanding what each requirement actually demands - and what role you play in satisfying it - is part of running a credible MSP practice in 2026.

  • SOC 2 client demands - what your clients need and what that requires of you
  • CMMC awareness for clients in the defense supply chain
  • Cyber insurance questionnaire support - for your clients, not just your own renewal
  • PIPEDA and Bill C-27 - privacy obligations for organizations you manage data for
  • AI governance for clients deploying AI tools without security review
