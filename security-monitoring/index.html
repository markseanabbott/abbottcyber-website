What this is - and what it isn't

Translation and accountability. Not log management.

I don't run your SOC. I don't sift through logs. I don't manage your SIEM configuration or tune your EDR rules. That's the vendor's job - and if they're not doing it, that's exactly the kind of thing I'll surface.

What I do: read the reports your security tools and vendors produce, interpret what they're telling you in plain language, and give leadership an honest picture of what's working and what isn't. If your SOC report says "no critical findings" and I'm looking at the same environment, I'll know whether that's reassuring or concerning.

The value is judgment and translation - someone with CISO-level experience who can sit between the technical output and the people who have to make decisions based on it.
