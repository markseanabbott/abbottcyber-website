Assessment types

The right framework for your situation

🛡

NIST CSF Assessment

The most complete risk picture. Covers Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover — mapping your current posture across all five functions and identifying the highest-priority gaps for your specific environment.

🎯

CIS Controls Assessment

Prioritized assessment against the 18 CIS Controls — starting with the foundational controls that deliver the most risk reduction. Particularly useful for organizations that want a concrete, actionable starting point.

💳

PCI DSS Readiness

Pre-QSA assessment for organizations processing payment card data. Scoping review, gap analysis, and remediation roadmap — so you know what you're walking into before the formal assessment begins.

🌐

ISO 27001 Pre-Assessment

Readiness review for organizations pursuing ISO 27001 certification. Identifies gaps in the ISMS structure, documentation, and controls before formal auditor engagement — reducing the cost and timeline of certification.

🔒

Cyber Insurance Readiness

Map your current controls against underwriter requirements before application or renewal. Identifies coverage-affecting gaps with time to address them — rather than discovering them at submission.

☁️

M365 / Cloud Posture Review

Microsoft 365 Secure Score audit and Azure configuration review — identifying misconfigured settings, unused security features, and identity hygiene gaps that most organizations don't know they have.

The process

How an assessment works

01

Discover

Understand your environment — technology stack, business operations, regulatory obligations, and current security controls. No assumptions. The assessment is scoped to your actual situation, not a generic template.

02

Assess

Structured evaluation against the selected framework — interviews, documentation review, configuration checks. Every finding is validated before it makes it into the report.

03

Report

A clear gap analysis report with findings written for both technical and executive audiences. No padding, no vendor recommendations, no 200-page document with 180 pages of boilerplate.

04

Prioritize

Remediation recommendations sequenced by actual risk — what to do first, what to do next, and what can wait. Budget-aware prioritization, not a list that assumes infinite resources.

What you get

Every assessment produces documents you can act on — not a report that confirms what you already suspected without telling you what to do about it.
